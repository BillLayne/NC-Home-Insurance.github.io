Frequently Asked Questions

What does standard home insurance NOT cover?

Standard policies typically exclude floods, earthquakes, maintenance issues, pest infestations, and intentional damage. Flood insurance requires a separate policy through NFIP or private insurers.

How much dwelling coverage do I need?

You need enough to rebuild your home at current construction costs, not market value. This includes materials, labor, and compliance with current building codes. Most experts recommend coverage of at least 100% of rebuild cost.

What's the difference between replacement cost and actual cash value?

Replacement cost pays to replace damaged items with new ones, while actual cash value deducts depreciation. For a 5-year-old TV, replacement cost covers a new TV, while ACV pays the depreciated value.

Do I need flood insurance in North Carolina?

If you're in a high-risk flood zone with a mortgage, flood insurance is required. Even outside flood zones, 25% of flood claims come from low-risk areas. NC's hurricane risk makes flood coverage worth considering.
